3 · Statistical Details

3.1 · Sharpe Ratio Methodology

For ranking models, Sharpe ratio is computed as: Sharpe_ann = (mean_ls_ret / σ_car) × √(12/h) where mean_ls_ret is the mean long-short abnormal return (long top-3, short bottom-3), σ_car is the standard deviation of cross-sectional CARs, and h is the horizon in months. This annualizes but does not adjust for overlapping-return autocorrelation.

3.2 · Classification Edge (Regression Models)

For regression models, "edge" measures predictive accuracy net of the naive baseline: edge = P(sign(prediction) = sign(true_CAR)) - P(naive majority-class prediction is correct). Negative edge indicates systematic anti-correctness; near-zero edge indicates random predictions.
